Invariant is built on one locked concept. Everything else follows from it.

The Load-Bearing Artefact

An artefact becomes load-bearing when two conditions are met simultaneously:

It persists under reuse. The same dataset, signal, or parameter is relied upon repeatedly — not just once.
Removing it causes material consequences. If invalidated or substituted, a meaningful portion of exposure is affected.

Artefacts that persist without being tested are labelled: persistent but untested. This is not failure — it is information about current exposure.

Research Constitution

Five principles.
Locked.

Invariant survives success, politics, and time because it does not ask for virtue, acknowledgement, or confession.

1

Memory must resist reinterpretation. Structure beats prose. Dependence is observed, not reported.

2

Evidence must bind success as tightly as failure. Wins rewrite history more aggressively than losses.

3

No decision is blocked. But no important decision is forgettable.

4

Influence is acknowledged and constrained. Nothing is hidden. Nothing is silent.

5

Invariant never comforts. It confronts reality under power.
